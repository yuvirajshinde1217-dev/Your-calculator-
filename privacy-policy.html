Hum apne users se koi bhi personal information (jaise naam, email, ya phone number) collect nahi karte hain. Ye ek simple tool hai jo aapke browser mein chalta hai.

Hum kisi bhi tarah ki tracking cookies ka istemal nahi karte jo aapki personal activity ko track kare.

Agar hum ads dikhate hain, toh wo sirf user experience ko behtar banane ke liye basic technical data (jaise device type) ka istemal kar sakte hain.

Chonki hum koi data store hi nahi karte, isliye aapka data hamare paas poori tarah surakshit hai.

Agar aapke koi sawal hain, toh aap humse contact us page ke zariye pooch sakte hain.
