Privacy Policy

Hum aapki privacy ka samman karte hain. Hamara calculator app koi bhi personal data collect nahi karta hai.

1. Data Collection

Hum apne users se koi bhi sensitive information jaise naam, email ya phone number store nahi karte.

2. Cookies

Hum behtar user experience ke liye browser cookies ka istemal kar sakte hain.

3. Third-party Ads

Hum ads dikhane ke liye third-party ad networks ka istemal kar sakte hain jo aapke device ki basic jankari (jaise IP address) use kar sakte hain.

Agar aapke koi sawal hain, toh aap humse sampark kar sakte hain.
